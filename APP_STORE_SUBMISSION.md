## Apple App Store (iOS)

### Prerequisites
- Apple Developer Account ($99/year)
- App Store Connect access
- Xcode 14+
- TestFlight build ready

### App Store Listing

#### App Name
```
I Am AI Ready
```

#### Subtitle
```
Declare Your AI Identity
```

#### Description
```
Join the global "Are You AI Ready?" movement and declare your AI identity.

I Am AI Ready is your platform to:

✓ Choose from 8 AI identity filters representing your role in the AI era
✓ Record a 30-60 second video declaration of your AI readiness
✓ Share your declaration on LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and Instagram
✓ Connect with a global community of AI-ready professionals
✓ Access curated events, masterclasses, and leadership programs
✓ Compete for monthly and annual prizes

Features:
• Native video recording with Snapchat-style animated text overlays
• 8 unique AI identity filters (AI Ready, AI Savvy, AI Accountable, AI Driven, AI Enabler, Building Institutions, Leading Responsibly, Shaping Ecosystems)
• Inline video playback and editing
• Calendar export for registered events
• Community voting and expert judging
• Real-time notifications for contest updates
• Offline video recording capability

The "Are You AI Ready?" campaign runs through November 2026, with monthly winners announced and annual grand prizes awarded in December.

Positioned by DS Consortium to advance AI readiness across institutions globally.
```

#### Keywords
```
AI, artificial intelligence, readiness, declaration, video, contest, community, education, professional development, innovation
```

https://legroupeds.com/support

### Content Rating Questionnaire
- **Gambling:** No
- **Contests, Lotteries, Betting:** Yes (contest with prizes)
- **Alcohol, Tobacco, Drugs:** No
- **Mature Content:** No
- **Violence:** No
- **Profanity:** No
- **Sexual Content:** No
- **Medical Information:** No
- **Horror/Fear Themes:** No
- **Cartoon Violence:** No
- **Unrestricted Web Access:** No

### Submission Steps
1. Create app record in App Store Connect
2. Fill in app information and metadata
3. Upload build via Xcode or Transporter
4. Add screenshots and preview video
5. Complete content rating questionnaire
6. Submit for review (typically 24-48 hours)

## Testing Checklist

Before submission, verify:

- [ ] App launches without crashes
- [ ] All features work correctly
- [ ] Video recording works on various devices
- [ ] Video playback is smooth
- [ ] Moderation system functions properly
- [ ] Calendar export works for all formats
- [ ] Sharing to social media works
- [ ] Offline functionality works
- [ ] Battery usage is reasonable
- [ ] App size is acceptable
- [ ] All links are functional
- [ ] Privacy policy is accessible
- [ ] No sensitive data is logged
- [ ] Permissions are justified
- [ ] UI is responsive on all screen sizes
